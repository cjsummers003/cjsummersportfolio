My Story

...how it all started

My coding journey started as a way to promote my professional athletic career, but turned into a passion. For years I've had an active interest in the web and modern technology. Being fascinated with its continuous fast growth and knew it was something I wanted to be a part of.

I enjoy learning and creating something out of nothing by mixing up my knowledge, skills and imagination. So in 2018 I enrolled into the UCF Full Stack Bootcamp and the more I learned the more my passion grew. Now I am looking to start a career where I can be both creative and continue to learn and grow.

Coding

...a few words about

I enjoy the process of building applications from scratch. Solving problems and finding the best solutions is a creative process which often pushes us leaving our comfort zone.

I chose Front End Development because it allows me to work closely with design. Having background in television production and creating digital video content I am pulled towards visual aspect of web development. It's amazing to be able to take a design and turn it into a functional product for people to use and enjoy.

All my projects are fully responsive. Turning my work into a good user experience on mobile devices is my priority and I take a great care of it.
